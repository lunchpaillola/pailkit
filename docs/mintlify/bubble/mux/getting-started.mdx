The plugin is part of the [Video, Audio & Live Streaming Bundle](/bubble/video-audio-live-bundle/getting-started) and allows you to create live streams through Mux.

![Mux Plugin](https://res.cloudinary.com/dpfmaccxx/image/upload/v1731603317/MUX_pjk1v7.png)

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Go to the [Bubble Plugin
    Marketplace](https://bubble.io/plugin/video-audio--live-bundle-1727441795463x240182085842370560)
    and install the plugin into your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify installation">
    After installation, verify that the plugin appears in your list of installed
    plugins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your Mux credentials">
    In your Mux account, go to **Settings** and select **Access Tokens**.
    Generate a new token with **Mux Video** permissions. Save the **Access Token
    ID** and **Secret Key** provided.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the plugin in Bubble">
    In your Bubble app, go to **Plugins** and find the **Video, Audio & Live
    Streaming Bundle** plugin. Enter the **Access Token ID** and **Secret Key**
    from the previous step into the **Mux Token ID** and **Mux Token Secret**
    fields.
  </Step>
</Steps>
