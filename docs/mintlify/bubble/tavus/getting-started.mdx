The plugin is part of the [Video, Audio & Live Streaming Bundle](/bubble/video-audio-live-bundle/getting-started) and enables replicas and AI bots within Daily video calls.

![Tavus Plugin](https://res.cloudinary.com/dpfmaccxx/image/upload/v1732139310/tavusintheplugin_klider.gif)

## Installation

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the plugin">
    Go to the [Bubble Plugin
    Marketplace](https://bubble.io/plugin/video-audio--live-bundle-1727441795463x240182085842370560)
    and install the plugin into your app.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify installation">
    After installation, verify that the plugin appears in your list of installed
    plugins.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add your Tavus credentials">
    In the [api keys tab of your Tavus
    account](https://platform.tavus.io/api-keys) **create new key**
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure the plugin in Bubble">
    In your Bubble app, go to **Plugins** and find the **Video, Audio & Live
    Streaming Bundle** plugin. Enter the API key in the **Tavus API Key** field.
  </Step>
</Steps>
